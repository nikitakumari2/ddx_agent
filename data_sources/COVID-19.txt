A contagious respiratory disease caused by infection with the SARS-CoV-2 virus. It can lead to a wide range of symptoms and complications, from mild respiratory illness to severe disease and death. Common Symptoms: Fever or chills, cough, shortness of breath or difficulty breathing, fatigue, muscle or body aches, headache, new loss of taste or smell, sore throat, congestion or runny nose, nausea or vomiting, and diarrhea.
