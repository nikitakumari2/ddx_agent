Depression is a mood disorder that causes a persistent feeling of sadness and loss of interest. Also called major depressive disorder or clinical depression, it affects how you feel, think and behave and can lead to a variety of emotional and physical problems. Common Symptoms: Persistent sad, anxious, or "empty" mood; feelings of hopelessness; irritability; loss of interest in hobbies; fatigue; changes in appetite or sleep patterns; thoughts of death or suicide.
