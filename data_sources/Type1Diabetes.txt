 A chronic condition, thought to be an autoimmune reaction, in which the pancreas produces little or no insulin. Insulin is a hormone needed to allow sugar (glucose) to enter cells to produce energy. Without it, glucose builds up in the bloodstream. Common Symptoms: Symptoms often develop rapidly and include increased thirst (polydipsia), frequent urination (polyuria), extreme hunger (polyphagia), unintended weight loss, fatigue and weakness, and blurred vision.
