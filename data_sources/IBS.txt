Irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) is a common disorder that affects the large intestine. Signs and symptoms include cramping, abdominal pain, bloating, gas, and diarrhea or constipation, or both. IBS is a chronic condition that you'll need to manage long term. Common Symptoms: Abdominal pain or cramping (often related to passing a bowel movement); a change in the appearance of bowel movements; a change in how often you are having a bowel movement; bloating; gas; mucus in the stool.
