An infection or inflammation of the middle ear, the air-filled space located behind the eardrum. It is extremely common in young children due to the size and shape of their eustachian tubes. Common Symptoms: Ear pain (otalgia) is the primary symptom. Other signs include a feeling of fullness in the ear, muffled hearing, fluid drainage from the ear canal, and fever. In infants and toddlers, signs may include tugging at the ear, increased irritability, and difficulty sleeping.
